Automated Anatomical Labeling (AAL) Atlas

The AAL atlas segments the brain into 116 anatomical regions, useful for different types of functional and anatomical analysis with neuroimaging data. The atlas is overlayed on a standard MNI152 anatomical template, and different thresholds correspond to different anatomical regions. Use the bar to scroll through these regions, and (coming soon) view the corresponding AAL label.
